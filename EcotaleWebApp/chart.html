Logistics Supply Chain Process

🌍
Origin

Goods are sourced or produced at the origin location.

🛣️
Planning

Determine best route and mode of transport.

📋
Documentation

Ensure all documents are prepared and accurate.

📦
Packaging

Ensure goods are packaged and labelled correctly.

🚢
Transportation

Work with freight forwarders and carriers to handle logistics.

📑
Customs Compliance

Submit documents and ensure goods comply with regulations.

📬
Handling & Delivery

Ensure goods reach the destination and clear customs.

📍
Tracking & Monitoring

Real-time tracking and communication.

🛡️
Insurance

Purchase insurance to mitigate risk.

📞
Post-Delivery

Address any issues and collect feedback.
